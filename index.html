Things I'm Thinking About

What does it mean for gapless systems to have edge modes? Are there bulk invariants that can detect them?

How can we leverage measurement and feedback to produce interesting quantum states?

How do we describe "self-dual" critical points such as in the Fradkin-Shenker phase diagram?

What are the observable consequences of higher Berry phase?

What are the categorical symmetries of 3d TQFT? Is there an extension theory of fusion categories? How do we understand bulk-boundary correspondence for 1-form symmetries and their generalizations?

Related to the above, are QCAs trivial in the realm of local Hamiltonian evolution? Is there "quantum" L-theory?

Sometimes I shout other random ideas into the void at @ranglelangle.
